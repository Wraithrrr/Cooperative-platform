What is Musharakah?

Musharakah is a joint investment partnership where both BCS and members contribute capital towards a business venture or asset acquisition. This partnership model ensures fair profit sharing and risk distribution.

Profit Sharing

Profits are distributed based on pre-agreed ratios between BCS and member.

Loss Distribution

Losses are shared proportionally according to capital contribution.

Ownership Transfer

Gradual transfer of ownership to the member over the partnership period.

Transparent Terms

Clear documentation of partnership terms and conditions.
